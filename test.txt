Here are three workout sessions for one week focusing on chest, biceps, and back:

Day 1: Chest
1. Chest Press: 3 sets x 10 reps
2. Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 12 reps
3. Cable Flyes: 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Push-Ups: 3 sets to failure

Day 2: Biceps
1. Barbell Curl: 3 sets x 10 reps
2. Hammer Curls: 3 sets x 12 reps
3. Concentration Curls: 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Chin-Ups: 3 sets x failure

Day 3: Back
1. Deadlifts: 3 sets x 8 reps
2. Bent Over Rows: 3 sets x 10 reps
3. Pull-Ups: 3 sets x failure
4. Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets x 12 reps

Remember to incorporate a proper warm-up and cool-down routine before and after each workout session to prevent injury and optimize performance. Adjust weights and repetitions based on individual fitness levels and goals.
